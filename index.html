Prototype workshop · AI audiovisual creation

Turning images, voices, and dramatic ideas into film prototypes with AI.

CineAI Lab is an experimental creation workshop born through the use of ChatGPT, Codex and Blender. It explores how an independent creator can move from visual concepts and dialogue to a demonstrable audiovisual prototype.

View the OpenAI request Explore the film tests

Core experiment

Can AI help non-technical creators build short film prototypes without forcing them to become software engineers?

01 · The tool

An AI workshop for early-stage film creation.

CineAI Lab is not presented as a finished product. It is a working prototype designed to test a simple creative workflow: writing, image generation, image animation, scene continuity, synthetic voices, and basic production assembly.

The aim is to build a small but usable environment where ChatGPT, Blender supports creative direction and Codex helps assemble a practical production workflow.

02 · Film tests

Two concrete projects guide the prototype.

Film Test 1

The Memory of the Elephant

A visual short film project about animal memory, fear, and inner perception. It tests whether AI can create poetic visual continuity from still images.

  • Visual memory and dreamlike sequences
  • Animation of still images
  • Textures, atmosphere, and color transitions
  • Animal motion: the walking movement of an elephant and its herd, the movement of a mouse, and horror scenes of hyenas chasing and persecuting theirs preys.
Film Test 2

The Father

A contemporary adaptation inspired by August Strindberg. It tests whether AI can support dramatic continuity, dialogue, synthetic voices, and staging across a longer narrative.

  • Contemporary adaptation of a classic text
  • Dramatic continuity across scenes
  • Synthetic voices in Swedish and French
  • Character consistency over time

03 · Request to OpenAI

Extended experimental access to Codex for 6 to 8 weeks.

CineAI Lab is seeking extended experimental access to Codex in order to finalize a demonstrable prototype of an AI-assisted audiovisual creation workflow.

The goal is not only to generate isolated images or scripts, but to test how ChatGPT and Codex can work together as a creative and technical pair: one guiding the audiovisual intention, the other helping build the working tool.

Vision

A bridge between imagination and production.

CineAI Lab begins with two film tests. The broader ambition is to understand whether this approach could become a creative tool or service for filmmakers, writers, artists, and small production teams.
